# Market Brief: Portuguese (pt) — Brazil-leaning, Portugal-compatible

## Buyer persona

Brazilian brand owners, promotional-products companies (brindes), and
sourcing managers; secondary audience in Portugal. Brazil has a large local
bag industry (Danka, Roma Confeccoes) — buyers comparing overseas OEM
against local production care about landed cost, import logistics, and MOQ.

## What Portuguese-speaking buyers care about (priority order)

1. **Orcamento rapido** — "solicitar orcamento" is THE standard B2B CTA in
   Brazil (preferred over "cotacao" in this industry); 24-hour response is
   a strong differentiator.
2. **Custo total competitivo** — Myanmar price advantage framed against
   both China sourcing and local BR production; mention FOB/CIF clearly.
3. **Qualidade verificavel** — ISO 9001 recognized (local leaders certify);
   Japanese-brand track record signals discipline.
4. **Pedido minimo (MOQ)** — flexible MOQ matters; local BR factories offer
   low minimums, so explain the value at our MOQ tier.
5. **Personalizacao completa** — full custom (Pantone, materiais, ferragens).

## Language and register

- Brazil-leaning neutral: voce/sua empresa; avoid tu; forms readable in
  Portugal (avoid heavy BR slang; "equipe" ok, prefer neutral phrasing).
- Industry vocabulary: fabrica de mochilas personalizadas, fabricacao OEM,
  orcamento (quote), prazo de entrega/producao (lead time), amostra (sample),
  amostragem (sampling), tecido, ferragens (hardware), controle de qualidade
  (QC), marca propria (private label), pedido minimo (MOQ) — first mention
  "pedido minimo (MOQ)", then MOQ; solucao completa (one-stop).
- Keep OEM, ODM, MOQ, QC, Tech Pack (gloss "ficha tecnica" on first
  long-form mention), AQL, FOB/CIF/EXW, YKK, Pantone in Latin abbreviations.
- Proper accents/cedilla mandatory (fabrica->fábrica, orcamento->orçamento,
  producao->produção in actual output).
- Numbers keep source values identical.

## Tone

Direto e cordial — benefit-led, concrete, professional warmth typical of
Brazilian B2B copy, without exclamation marks.
