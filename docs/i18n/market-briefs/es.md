# Market Brief: Spanish (es) — Spain + Latin America (neutral)

## Buyer persona

Spanish-speaking brand owners, promotional-products distributors
(mayoristas/promocionales), and sourcing managers across Spain, Mexico,
and South America. Many are diversifying away from pure-China sourcing.
Reference points: europages.es, fabricantes de mochilas personalizadas,
mayoreo B2B (MX).

## What Spanish-speaking buyers care about (priority order)

1. **Precio competitivo con calidad verificable** — the Myanmar cost
   advantage is the hook; back it immediately with QC process and
   certifications so it does not read as "barato".
2. **Cotización rápida y clara** — fast, structured quoting (24 horas) is a
   differentiator; "cotización" is the standard term (not "presupuesto" in
   B2B manufacturing context).
3. **Flexibilidad de MOQ** — important for growing LatAm brands.
4. **Track record internacional** — Japanese-brand partnerships signal
   world-class QC; recognizable brands (Anello) help.
5. **Logística** — FOB/CIF terms, reliable lead times to both Europe and
   the Americas.

## Language and register

- Neutral international Spanish: ustedes, never vosotros; no regional slang;
  vocabulary understandable in both Spain and LatAm.
- Industry vocabulary: fabricante de mochilas, fabricación/producción OEM,
  cotización (quote), plazo de entrega (lead time), muestra (sample),
  muestreo (sampling), tejido/material, herrajes (hardware), control de
  calidad (QC), marca propia (private label), solución integral (one-stop),
  personalización (customization).
- Keep OEM, ODM, MOQ, QC, Tech Pack (gloss "ficha técnica" on first
  long-form mention), AQL, FOB/CIF/EXW, YKK, Pantone in Latin abbreviations.
- "mochila" (not "morral"); "bolso"/"bolsa" for generic bags per context.
- Typography: proper tildes and opening ¿ ¡ mandatory; numbers keep source
  values identical.

## Tone

Cercano pero profesional — direct, benefit-led, concrete numbers; slightly
warmer than the German version but still B2B sober. No exclamation marks.
